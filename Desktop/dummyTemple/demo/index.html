Sri Ramatheertham Temple

Ramateertham is a village panchayat in Nellimarla mandal of Vizianagaram district in Andhra Pradesh in India. It is about 12 km from Vizianagaram city. It is a famous pilgrimage site and an ancient historical site dating back to the 3rd Century BCE. There is a post office at Ramateertham with the PIN code 535218.

Festivals Celebrated

  • Vaikuntha Ekadasi:
  • Every year, Ramateertham Giripradarshana is organized during Vaikuntha Ekadasi.
  • Full Moon Days
  • Navaratri
  • Shivaratri
  • Sri Ramanavami

The Sri Ramatheertham Temple is renowned for the vibrant festivals it hosts throughout the year. The most significant celebration is during Vaikuntha Ekadasi, where thousands of devotees take part in the annual Giripradarshana. Other major festivals include Navaratri, celebrated with great zeal, Shivaratri, which is dedicated to Lord Shiva, and Sri Ramanavami, commemorating the birth of Lord Rama. On these occasions, the temple is adorned with lights, traditional decorations, and special rituals that draw people from all over the region. Full Moon Days are also considered auspicious, where special prayers are held in the temple.

Santhana Venugopala Swamy Temple

The Santhana Venugopala Swamy Temple is a revered temple dedicated to Lord Krishna in his form as Santhana Venugopala Swamy, worshiped as the divine protector of children and families. This form of Krishna holds a flute, symbolizing his role as a divine herdsman who brings harmony and joy to life. The temple is known for its significance in blessing devotees with progeny, making it a popular destination for those praying for children or the welfare of their family. The name "Santhana" is associated with progeny and offspring, and the deity here is believed to bless childless couples with the gift of children. The temple draws in a large number of devotees, especially couples who seek the blessings of Lord Krishna for fertility and familial happiness. The peaceful atmosphere of the temple, combined with the compassionate image of Venugopala Swamy, offers comfort to all who visit.

About Our Temples

The temples in the Nellimarla constituency are a testament to the region's rich cultural and spiritual heritage. Each temple has its own unique history, architecture, and traditions that have been preserved and celebrated for centuries. From the serene surroundings of the Venugopala Swamy Temple, dedicated to Lord Krishna, to the powerful and revered Pydithalli Ammavaru Temple in Vizianagaram, these sacred sites offer a deep connection to the divine.

Visitors to Nellimarla will find a diverse array of temples, each with its own story to tell. Whether you are seeking spiritual enlightenment, historical knowledge, or simply a peaceful retreat, the temples of Nellimarla provide a profound experience. Our platform aims to bring all these temples under one digital roof, making it easy for devotees and visitors to explore and appreciate the rich history and traditions of these holy places.
